Sigma

by ThinkingTransistor

The first easy to understand building block system for automating intelligent behavior.

Machine Intelligence made easy and accessible
Sigma is designed for anyone, regardless of prior experience. Sigma automatically takes care of tedious tasks like differentiation and dataset transformation. Want to save training progress and continue another day? Or just export your model to another machine? A single line of code is all it takes.

Unlike other frameworks, Sigma is not just for experienced programmers. Most operations can also be performed solely using the provided user interface, making building artificial intelligence as easy as drafting a spreadsheet.
